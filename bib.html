To make this website, I took the idea of my paper, which is that loot boxes are bad, and transferred it into a short summary with some supplemental links and images. To do this I used basic html and css formatting, and some basic images and gifs from youtube and google. I chose this particularly basic color theme, because the two colors most associated with gambling are black and red, and I thought the basic colors were easier to work with.
